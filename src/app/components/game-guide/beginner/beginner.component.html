Beginner's Guide

Upon creation of your character, you'll be spawned in Loa Castle.

Start with opening your Beginner Welcome Package and get the following items:

  • Super Noble HP/MP
  • Beginner's EXP Talisman (7 days)
  • IB Rank C Package (3 pcs)

As you level, you'll get more beginner items:

  • x2 Armlet / 1 Belt
  • x2 Earrings
  • x3 Rings, 1 Main Ring (Good for PVE and valid for 7 days)

Access Dshop and get your mount, pet and portal scroll (These are free).

Note: Staying online, gives you 4 coins/hour.
