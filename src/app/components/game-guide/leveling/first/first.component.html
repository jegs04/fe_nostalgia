Level

First and foremost is to equip your EXP talisman, then eliminate monsters outside of Loa Castle until you reach level 12. (At this level, you'll be able to teleport to Deneb)

Once you're in Deneb, eliminate monsters until you reach level 25. (At this level, you'll be able to teleport to Parca Temple)

Things to consider:

  • Akris gives a lot of experience but don't force yourself to find one. Eliminate monsters as much as possible.
  • Don't forget to loot. You'll need dils to buy your skills

Once you're in Parca Temple, proceed in this area and eliminate monsters until you reach level 44. (At this level, you'll be able to teleport to Norak Cave). Go back to Loa Castle and buy your skills.

Once you're in Norak Cave, create your own party and enter the instance dungeon. You must have the Infinite Battle Rank C tickets in order for you to enter.

Things to consider:

  • Set your heal to 100 (This can be changed and it's free)
  • Use your Super Noble HP/MP
  • Loot Incar's Medal
